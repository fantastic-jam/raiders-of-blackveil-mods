[size=5]DisableSkillsBar[/size]

Prevents the skills bar from reacting to accidental hover or clicks during combat.

By default, mousing over the skills bar while fighting unlocks your cursor and disables your attack input — a death sentence mid-fight. This mod blocks that behavior, including direct ability upgrades from clicking, unless you are deliberately holding [b]Alt[/b].

No configuration required.
