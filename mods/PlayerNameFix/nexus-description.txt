[size=5]PlayerNameFix[/size]

If your Steam profile is private, the game cannot read your display name during account creation and permanently stores [b]<N/A>[/b] on its backend. This mod overrides that with your actual Steam persona name — fixing both your in-game name seen by other players and the default party name in the host screen.

No configuration required.
