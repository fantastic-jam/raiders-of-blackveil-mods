[size=5]JoinAnytime[/size]

Lets a player join a session that is already mid-run. Instead of being turned away, they connect, wait out the current room, and spawn in at the start of the next room — with XP and perks averaged to match the existing players.

The existing players see no disruption. Enemy count, vote thresholds, and difficulty scaling are all based on the players currently in the world. The waiting joiner does not count until they actually spawn in.

[b]Only the host needs the mod.[/b] Clients without the mod installed still join and play normally — they receive perk choice pickups instead of shrine rooms. Unmodded clients may see console errors during the waiting phase, but these are non-breaking and stop once the joiner spawns in. Installing the mod on joining clients eliminates these errors entirely.

[line]
[heading]How it works[/heading]

[list]
[*]The joining player connects and waits — no champion, no presence in the world.[/*]
[*]When the current room ends and the next room loads, they spawn in normally.[/*]
[*]Their champion receives floor-averaged XP and rarity-matched random perks from the existing players.[/*]
[/list]
