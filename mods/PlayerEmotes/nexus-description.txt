[size=5]EmoteMod[/size]

Send floating emoji reactions visible to all players in the session. Press a configurable keybind to trigger an emote that appears above your character and is broadcast to everyone in the run.

[line]

[heading]Requirements[/heading]
[list]
[*][url=https://store.steampowered.com/app/2246780/Raiders_of_Blackveil/]Raiders of Blackveil[/url]
[*][url=https://github.com/BepInEx/BepInEx/releases]BepInEx 5[/url]
[/list]

[line]

[heading]Installation[/heading]

[b]1. Install BepInEx[/b]

Skip this step if BepInEx is already installed.
[list]
[*]Download [b]BepInEx 5[/b] from the [url=https://github.com/BepInEx/BepInEx/releases]BepInEx releases page[/url] — pick the [b]BepInEx_win_x64[/b] build.
[*]Extract the contents into your game folder (the one containing [b]RoB.exe[/b]).
[*]Launch the game once and close it — BepInEx will initialize its folder structure.
[/list]

[b]2. Install the mod[/b]
[list]
[*]Download [b]EmoteMod-x.x.x.zip[/b] from the [url=https://github.com/fantastic-jam/raiders-of-blackveil-mods/releases?q=EmoteMod]releases page[/url].
[*]Extract the ZIP into your game's [b]BepInEx[/b] folder.
[*]Launch the game.
[/list]
