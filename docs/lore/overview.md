# Raiders of Blackveil — World Overview

> Sources: Steam store page, Game Rant interviews with director Christopher Nelson and CEO Janos Flösser, PCGamesN reveal article, Worthplaying coverage. The game entered Steam Early Access on December 15, 2025, and lore details are still expanding.

---

## The World: Wildguard

The game takes place in **Wildguard**, described by the developers as a "light-hearted but also dystopian fantasy world." Humans and anthropomorphic animals coexist in this setting — but not harmoniously. The world blends dark industrial themes with whimsical fantasy aesthetics, and the developers have cited the tone as balancing "dark and whimsical humor with awesome fantasy."

Comparisons to the *Sonic the Hedgehog* Archie comic/Saturday-morning-cartoon tradition have been drawn by the developers themselves, though they are explicit that Raiders of Blackveil tells its own original story.

---

## The Central Conflict

The dominant power in Wildguard is **Blackveil**, a human industrial mega-corporation that has enslaved nature and oppresses anthropomorphic animals for profit. Blackveil enforces what the developers describe as "a mindless consumer society" — industrialised mass production, environmental destruction, and the exploitation of animal labour.

The rebellion fighting Blackveil is the player's faction. Organised animal rebels conduct surgical strikes against Blackveil installations across the world: stealing resources, sabotaging operations, and freeing prisoners. The stated long-term goal of the rebellion is to "take down the big bad CEO of Blackveil" — an unnamed figure who serves as the ultimate antagonist of the campaign.

---

## Tone and Themes

- **Anti-corporate resistance**: the rebellion is explicitly framed as fighting greedy industrialism.
- **Solidarity**: co-op play and the shared base (The Liberator) reinforce collective action as a theme.
- **Reclaiming nature**: Blackveil's factories represent the despoiling of the natural world; raiding them is an act of both sabotage and reclamation.
- **Dark humour**: the game does not take itself too seriously — the first operation site is a low-quality industrial meat factory, and enemy names such as "Meat Delivery" and "Puke Sweep" lean into the absurd.

---

## Developer Context

Developed by **Wombo Games**, a studio co-founded by:
- **Janos Flösser** (CEO) — co-founder of IO Interactive (*Hitman* series)
- **Christopher Nelson** (Game Director)

Offices in Copenhagen and Budapest. Raiders of Blackveil is Wombo Games' debut title. The concept emerged from the team playing games together for years and searching for "this very particular experience" they could not find elsewhere.

---

## Current State (Early Access)

Raiders of Blackveil launched on Steam Early Access on **December 15, 2025**. At launch the game contained:

- 4 playable champions (with 4 more planned)
- 2 operation sites / biomes: the **Meat Factory** and the **Harvest Operation**
- 8 character classes, 300+ perks
- 1–3 player co-op

The full version is expected to add more champions, more operation sites, and a complete narrative arc ending with confronting Blackveil's CEO.
